Representative Publications

Targeting keystone species restores butyrate-producing bacteria in NAFLD

Introduced a causal inference algorithm to identify keystone species in gut microbiome, demonstrating their potential as therapeutic targets for non‑alcoholic fatty liver disease.

Dr AFC: drug repositioning through anti‑fibrosis characteristics

Built a knowledge‑based machine learning framework that integrates structural and biological profiles to predict anti‑fibrosis drugs, validated across multiple external datasets.

Phylogenetic analyses place mitochondria within Alphaproteobacteria

Systematically addressed phylogenetic artefacts and provided robust evidence that mitochondria share a common ancestor with Rickettsiales and marine alphaproteobacterial lineages.

Deep learning for metagenomic biomarker discovery

Developed a convolutional neural network that accurately identifies microbial biomarkers from shotgun metagenomic data, improving early diagnosis of inflammatory bowel disease.
